🫥 Blind Hiring

Eliminating recruitment bias in Nasugbu. Anonymized profiles ensure every applicant is evaluated purely on skills and merit.

Fair Hiring Starts with Anonymity

TalentScout AI removes personal identifiers — name, gender, age, photo, and address — from all applications before they reach employers. Hiring decisions are based solely on skills, experience, and qualifications.

✓ No Name Revealed ✓ No Photo ✓ No Address ✓ No Age / Gender
40%
Reduction in bias-based rejections
2.3×
More diverse hires
The Process

How Blind Hiring Works

1
Applicant Submits

The applicant fills in their complete profile including skills, experience, and education details.

2
System Anonymizes

TalentScout AI automatically strips away all personally identifiable information before forwarding to employers.

3
Employer Reviews

Employer sees only Applicant ID, skills, experience level, and match score — nothing personal.

4
Identity Revealed

Only after shortlisting and upon applicant consent is identity revealed for the interview stage.
